In the last three decades, Plasel has accumulated significant expertise in designing and manufacturing diverse plastic products, utilizing a variety of materials.

Creativity & Innovation

At Plasel, we pride ourselves on our creative and innovative product designs, alongside our efficient process utilization.

Leading in Injection Molding Technology

We specialize in double component products and excel in combining materials using advanced insert mold and over mold technologies.

J.I.T. Deliveries All Around the Globe

Our global infrastructure ensures J.I.T. (Just-In-Time) deliveries to customers across Europe, North America, and Asia, providing timely and efficient service worldwide.

Flexible Thinking and Performance

Our flexibility allows us to adapt to our customers' needs, ensuring that we provide tailored solutions that meet their unique requirements.

Passion in Everything We Do – We Strive for Excellency

Our motivated employees are passionate about serving our customers optimally and are committed to continual improvement in everything we do.

Fairness and Transparency with All Our Business Associates

Our relationships with business associates are built on fairness and transparency, fostering trust and collaboration, both within our organization and with customers and suppliers abroad.

Emphasis on Quality

Quality is ingrained in our culture, beginning with product designs and extending through every stage of our operations, ensuring customers can trust our products for continuous use.

Adopting Sustainability as a Way of Life

At Plasel, sustainability is not just a core value but a way of life. We are dedicated to creating a better world for future generations.
