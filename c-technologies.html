Plasel is a leading global plastic factory equipped with advanced blow molding and injection molding technologies, enabling a versatile range of innovative products in addition to other services. From simple hollow items to intricate, precise components, our experience and technologies deliver the flexibility and efficiency needed to cater diverse industries and applications. Our ability to handle various materials and complex designs ensures we are well-equipped to support large-scale manufacturing needs and a wide variety of plastic parts and products.

Blow Molding

  1. Hollow Parts Production

    Ideal for creating hollow plastic products like bottles, containers, tanks, and hollow toys. The process involves extruding a tube of molten plastic (parison), which is then inflated into a mold to form the desired shape.

  2. Material Flexibility

    We can handle various plastics, including HDPE and PP, enabling the production of items with different material properties such as rigidity, transparency, or chemical resistance.

  3. High Volume Production

    Our process is efficient for large-scale production of lightweight and hollow items, suitable for mass production with consistent quality, making it cost-effective for high-volume manufacturing.

  4. Design Complexity

    We are capable of producing complex shapes with intricate details and uniform wall thickness, offering flexibility in creating unique designs for customized products.

  5. Diverse Applications

    Our products are widely used in the packaging industry for bottles and containers and in the automotive industry for air ducts, fluid reservoirs, connectors, and other components.

Injection Molding

  1. Precision & Accuracy

    Ideal for creating detailed and complex parts with high precision and tight tolerances. The process utilizes molds into which molten plastic is injected under high pressure, ensuring detailed replication of the mold design.

  2. Wide Range of Part Sizes

    Suitable for producing parts of various sizes, from tiny precision components to large structural elements.

  3. Design Complexity

    Capable of producing high volumes of identical parts quickly, reducing manufacturing costs through economies of scale and minimizing material waste with efficient use of plastic.

  4. Efficient Production

    Capable of producing high volumes of identical parts quickly, reducing manufacturing costs through economies of scale and minimizing material waste with efficient use of plastic.

  5. Diverse Applications

    Injection molding is widely used in the automotive, electronics, consumer goods, and medical device industries. Common applications include producing parts such as housings, gears, brackets, and medical syringes.

  6. Double Component

    We have vast experience and knowledge producing double component products. We are equipped with several double component machines that can produce variety of products, different sized and unlimited material selection.

Additional Services

With end-to-end manufacturing capabilities, Plasel offers a complete range of services from concept development and design to production and finishing, including post-production processes such as assembly, decoration, and packaging. Equipped with advanced quality control systems to ensure consistent product quality, we implement rigorous testing and inspection protocols to meet industry standards and customer requirements.

  1. Prototyping

    Our factory can quickly develop prototypes using either blow molding or injection molding techniques, allowing for rapid testing and iteration of designs, thereby reducing the time to market.

  2. Material Flexibility

    Compatible with a wide range of thermoplastics and thermosetting polymers, this method allows for the creation of parts with specific mechanical properties, including strength, flexibility, and temperature resistance.

  3. Custom Molding Solutions

    We design and manufacture custom molds tailored to specific product requirements, providing flexibility in creating unique and complex product designs.

  4. Material Selection & Testing

    We have expertise in selecting appropriate materials based on product requirements and performance specifications, and the capability to test and refine material properties to meet specific industry standards.

  5. Design & Engineering Support

    We offer comprehensive design support, including CAD modeling and simulation, and provide engineering expertise to optimize product design for manufacturability and performance.
