Smart Packaging

About Smart Packaging

Plasel is a global leader in plastic packaging solutions, with emphasize in the cutting tool industries.
Plasel are renowned for its expertise and innovation in plastic manufacturing. Our commitment to delivering creative solutions sets us apart in the industry, ensuring that every packaging need is met with precision and ingenuity. At Plasel, flexibility and optimal customer responsiveness are at the core of our service philosophy, allowing us to adapt to the unique requirements of each client swiftly and effectively.

Our Smart Packaging solutions are designed to exceed expectations, providing seamless JIT (Just-In-Time) deliveries to any corner of the globe. Whether you're looking for standard packaging or a bespoke solution, Plasel's comprehensive approach guarantees excellence in every product we deliver.

More About Smart Solutions

Building a global brand in plastic packaging

Plasel Plastics is a well-recognized brand in the industry, known especially for our smart innovation and expertise in cutting tools packaging. Boasting over three decades of packaging experience, combined with most advanced machinery and equipment.

Unrivaled flexible and professional services

We thrive on building long-lasting relationships with our customers. We know that in order to forge strong relationships, we need to extend flexible and professional service every step of the way. From our workforce to management, our skilled employees always aim to set the highest industry standard.

World-class logistics across the globe

We put a great deal of thought and consideration into every location, in aim to save our customers storage and shipping costs. In addition, our logistics facilities in Europe, Israel, Korea, China, India and the United States are strategically located to meet on-time delivery anywhere in the world.

Strict manufacturing compliance

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities are located in Israel’s Western Galilee and Southeast Asia, operating in full compliance with all regulatory requirements, including ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015 and ISO 45001:2018.
